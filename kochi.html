About Kochi

Kerala’s commercial capital mixes Fort Kochi’s colonial lanes with Ernakulam’s malls and metro. Infinity Routes lists boutique stays in Jew Town, business hotels on MG Road, and resort-style properties toward the backwater belt.

  • Cochin International Airport and rail links serve the city and cruise port.
  • Dec–Feb: driest months; monsoon Jun–Sep is lush but wet—boats still run on calmer days.
  • Famous for seafood, Ayurveda, and antique shops along Synagogue Road.
  • Extend to Alleppey houseboats or Munnar’s tea hills on longer itineraries.

Harbour light turns the nets gold every evening.

Top places to explore

Fort walks, spice air, and slow cruises through the backwaters.

Chinese fishing nets

Iconic cantilevered nets—best at golden hour with fresh catch stalls nearby.

Jew Town & Paradesi Synagogue

Antique shops and narrow lanes—closed Fridays for synagogue prayers.

Kathakali centres

Make-up demonstrations before performances—book seats in advance.

Backwater day cruises

Shorter trips from Marine Drive or full-day houseboats toward Alleppey.
