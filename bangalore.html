Quick facts about Bangalore

Bangalore is an Indian metropolis known as the Silicon Valley of India and the Garden City, combining a major information technology cluster with historic sites and parks. Infinity Routes lists accommodations across neighborhoods like Koramangala, Whitefield, and near Lalbagh Botanical Garden.

  • Bangalore hosts major IT hubs in Koramangala and Whitefield.
  • The city is noted as the Garden City with Lalbagh Botanical Garden and Cubbon Park.
  • The tech ecosystem fuels cafes, co-working spaces and networking events.
  • Top attractions include Bangalore Palace, Bannerghatta National Park, and NGMA.
  • Booking range: luxury to budget across neighborhoods and price points.
  • Transport: Bangalore Metro, buses, Uber, Ola, and bike rentals.
  • Best time to visit: Oct-Feb; summers can touch about 35C.
