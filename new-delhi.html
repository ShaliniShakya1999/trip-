About New Delhi

Lutyens Delhi, UNESCO sites, and busy markets sit side by side. Infinity Routes lists hotels in Connaught Place, Aerocity, Karol Bagh, and Greater Kailash for embassies, shopping, or heritage circuits.

  • Hubs: CP, Aerocity (IGI), NDLS, Kashmere Gate ISBT.
  • Metro plus app cabs; avoid peak ring-road hours.
  • Oct-Mar: clearest skies; Apr-Jun hot; Jul-Sep monsoon.
  • Day trips: Agra, Jaipur, Neemrana.

Wide boulevards and monuments shape the ceremonial heart.

Top places to explore

Mughal monuments, museums, and Old Delhi lanes.

India Gate and Rajpath

Evening lights and lawns - pair with nearby museums.

Qutub Minar

UNESCO complex with iron pillar and ruins.

Humayuns Tomb

Persian-inspired architecture in quiet gardens.

Chandni Chowk and Jama Masjid

Street food and wholesale bazaars - go hungry.

Lotus Temple

Bahai House of Worship - meditation-friendly halls.

Akshardham

Large temple complex with exhibitions - allow several hours.
